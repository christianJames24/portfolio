Recurring characters

  • Ciny

    Ciny is the first character that was created for this world. I've forgotten why his name was Ciny, but he is basically the protaganist for most of the comics he is featured in. I once went on google and searched up "Ciny" once and found an italian song that was named Ciny.

  • Steve

    Steve is Ciny's older brother. Unlike Ciny, I can clearly remember where his name came from. If you play minecraft then you could probably guess where the name comes from.

  • Girlo

    Girlo is the obligatory girl character. I made her because the comics didn't have a girl character. My sister draws this character more than me honestly. She made the picture for this because she's better at art.
